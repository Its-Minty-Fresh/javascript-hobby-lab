Toby the Dog!

Getting Started

Introduction

I don't have any really exciting hobbies, but I am proud of my dog Toby!

I adopted him a couple years ago from the Humane Society and have worked hard to train him well and teach him lots of tricks. he is great at loose leash walking (walking by your side without getting head of you or pulling on the leash), and can be off-leash since he has developed excellent recall.

In fact I did my Intro to Web Development Final Project on him just this past term!

Instructions

  1. Are you ready to adopt a dog?
  2. Make sure you have a plan!
  3. Make sure you understand this is a FOREVER commitment!

Did you know?

The Humane Society does not tell adopting parents the breed of the dog they are adopting. The reason being many people will refuse to adopt specific dogs based on the breed alone, not the personality and temperament of the dog. Other times they may not even know!
